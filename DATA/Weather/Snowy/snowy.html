Snowy Weather

Snowy weather is a meteorological condition where snowflakes fall from the sky, typically in cold climates during winter. The amount of snowfall can vary, ranging from a light dusting to heavy snowstorms. While it can be a beautiful sight, it can also pose hazards such as reduced visibility and slippery roads, increasing the risk of accidents. Therefore, it is important to take necessary precautions and measures to ensure safety and minimize disruption caused by snowy weather.
